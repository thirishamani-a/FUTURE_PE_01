# Google Ads vs SEO: Which Is Better for Business Growth?

Businesses often compare SEO and Google Ads when planning their marketing strategy.

## SEO

Advantages:

* Long-term results
* Organic traffic
* Better credibility

## Google Ads

Advantages:

* Instant visibility
* Quick lead generation
* Highly targeted campaigns

## Conclusion

The best approach is often a combination of SEO and Google Ads for balanced growth.
