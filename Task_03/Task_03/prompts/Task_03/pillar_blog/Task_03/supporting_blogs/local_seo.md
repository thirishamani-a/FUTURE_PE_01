# Local SEO Strategies to Rank Your Business on Google

Local SEO helps businesses attract customers within a specific geographic area.

## Key Strategies

### Optimize Google Business Profile

Keep business information updated and accurate.

### Use Local Keywords

Include city-specific keywords throughout website content.

### Collect Customer Reviews

Positive reviews improve local rankings and trust.

### Build Local Citations

List your business on trusted online directories.

## Conclusion

A strong local SEO strategy helps businesses improve visibility and attract more local customers.
